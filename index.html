About me

Hello! My name is Michelle, and I am here to help you figure out your life goals. I want to help mentor youth into figuring out their career, and life goals, from short term to long. I was once a teen, and I know it can be little difficult figuring out long term goals but it can be done, and I can help you do it. First I would start off figuring out who you are as you get to know me, and from there we will work together to figure out which path is the best for you. Doesn't hurt to get a little life advice right? I want to reinvent the box you have put into your mind. Let's not just think outside the box, but let's remodel it, add color, decorations, you. The key to a successful life is the people behind you, supporting you 100% I want to be the support you need.
